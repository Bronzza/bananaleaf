Our Story

Banana Leaf International Private Child Care was founded with a vision to provide exceptional early childhood education in a warm and nurturing environment. Our experienced team of educators is dedicated to helping children develop their full potential through play-based learning and personalized attention.

Our programs emphasize hands-on discovery, creativity, and emotional well-being. From engaging sensory play to outdoor exploration and themed learning, every activity is thoughtfully planned to support balanced growth. We also value strong partnerships with families, working closely together to ensure each child feels seen, supported, and celebrated every step of the way.
